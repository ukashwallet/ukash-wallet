Paysafecard to Alipay — FAQ

What is Alipay and how does it work?

Alipay is a third-party online payment platform launched by Ant Group (formerly Ant Financial), an affiliate of Alibaba Group. It supports payments at millions of merchants online and offline, QR code payments, money transfers, and financial products like savings and micro-loans.

Is Alipay available outside China?

Yes. Alipay International (the global version) is available in 90+ countries. Chinese tourists and expatriates worldwide also use the standard Alipay app, which increasingly supports international merchant payments.

What currency does Alipay use internationally?

Alipay International supports multiple currencies including EUR, GBP, USD, AUD, JPY, and others. The exact currency depends on your account region and the merchant or recipient.

Do I need to verify my Alipay account?

Basic Alipay accounts are available without full verification, but for receiving external transfers, a verified (real-name) Alipay account is typically required.

Minimum Paysafecard amount for Alipay?
