Paysafecard to XRP — FAQ

What is the 10 XRP reserve requirement?

Every XRP Ledger wallet must hold a minimum of 10 XRP as a network reserve. This prevents spam accounts and keeps the ledger healthy. These 10 XRP are permanently locked while the account exists — they cannot be sent, traded, or recovered.

What is a destination tag and why does it matter?

A destination tag is a number that identifies your account on an exchange. If the exchange requires one and you omit it, your XRP may be permanently lost. Always copy the destination tag from your exchange deposit page before sending.

How does XRP compare to Bitcoin for speed and cost?

XRP settles in 3–5 seconds vs Bitcoin's 10–60 minutes. XRP fees are ~0.00001 XRP (fractions of a cent) vs Bitcoin's variable fees that can reach $5–$50 during network congestion.

Is XRP legal to buy and hold in the UK/EU?

Yes. XRP is legal to buy, hold and sell in the UK and all EU member states.
