For people sending money to family in countries with volatile local currencies, USDT provides a stable store of value during transit.

USDT Network Selection Guide

TRC-20 for low fees

TRC-20 (Tron) is the cheapest network for USDT — fees typically $0.10-1.00. Best for transfers to Binance, OKX, Bybit, and most major exchanges that support TRC-20 deposits.

ERC-20 for DeFi

ERC-20 USDT is required for Ethereum DeFi protocols (Uniswap, Aave, Compound). Check gas fees on etherscan.io before transacting — off-peak hours (weekends, late night UTC) have lower fees.

Never mix networks

Sending ERC-20 USDT to a TRC-20 address (or vice versa) results in permanent loss. The address format may look identical — always confirm the network label in your wallet matches what you selected.
