For new Ethereum users, understanding gas fees is important: every Ethereum transaction requires ETH for gas (transaction processing fees). Fees vary from $0.50 during quiet periods to $50+ during peak demand. Check current gas prices on etherscan.io → Gas Tracker before making transactions, and consider Layer-2 networks like Arbitrum, Optimism, or Base for lower-cost DeFi activity — all of which use ETH for gas.

Ethereum Beginner's Checklist

MetaMask setup

MetaMask is the most widely supported Ethereum wallet with browser extension and mobile app. Download only from metamask.io — beware of fake MetaMask apps on app stores.

Gas fee timing

Ethereum gas fees are lowest on weekends and between 00:00-08:00 UTC. Check etherscan.io/gastracker for real-time fees before executing transactions.

Layer-2 for lower fees

For regular DeFi activity, bridge your ETH to Arbitrum or Base (Layer-2 networks) for 10-100x lower transaction fees while maintaining Ethereum-level security.
